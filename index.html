Understanding governance and the public good in uncertain times

The Future of Politics, led by the Department of Government at the University of Essex, seeks to study, understand and shape the future of politics in the United Kingdom and around the globe. Established in 2025, we are academics and practitioners working in politics and international relations using methodologically diverse approaches to understand how politics is changing, and how it can change for the better.
